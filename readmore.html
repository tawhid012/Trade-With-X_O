FAQ

What is profit sharing?

Answer: Profit sharing is a financial arrangement where a portion of a company's profits are distributed to its stakeholders.

How does profit sharing work in trading?

Answer: In trading, profit sharing involves a trader sharing a portion of their trading profits with investors who contribute capital.
